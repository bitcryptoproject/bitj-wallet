Have your Bits always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Bit Wallet is the first mobile Bit app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Bit amount in BIT, mBIT and µBIT.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Bit via NFC, QR-codes or Bit URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Bit addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Bit balance.

You can find more about Bit at
http://bit.io

If you want to contribute to Bit Wallet, the project is located at
